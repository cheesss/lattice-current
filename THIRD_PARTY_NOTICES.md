# Third-Party Notices

World Monitor relies on a mix of open-source libraries, public datasets, maps, and external information providers.

## Software Libraries

This repository includes or depends on third-party packages distributed under their own licenses. Review `package.json`, lockfiles, and upstream package metadata for complete dependency licensing.

Notable examples include:

- TypeScript
- Vite
- deck.gl
- MapLibre GL
- D3
- Three.js
- DuckDB
- Playwright
- Tauri

## Maps and Geospatial Attribution

Map and geospatial outputs may rely on providers such as:

- OpenStreetMap contributors
- CARTO basemaps
- Protomaps

Use of those providers remains subject to their attribution and licensing requirements.

## External Data and Content

World Monitor may integrate or reference data from:

- public RSS feeds
- public market and macroeconomic providers
- geospatial and infrastructure datasets
- open cyber threat feeds
- research and government sources

Those data providers keep their own rights and usage terms. Inclusion of connectors or references in this repository does not transfer ownership of the underlying content.

## Public Documentation Practice

The docs site and repository documentation should favor:

- project-authored summaries
- source attribution
- short quotations only when appropriate
- links to original sources
